NSLF-OL: Online Learning of Neural Surface Light Fields alongside Real-time Incremental 3D Reconstruction

Yijun Yuan1, Andreas Nüchter1,
1Julius-Maximilians-Universität Würzburg
Paper arXiv Code

We propose to learn NSLF alongside the real-time reconstruction. Our NSLF model is manipulated by Multiple Asynchronous Neural Agents (MANA) that roling data distribution and agent allocating. And thus, our NSLF is unbounded and thus has potential to work in arbitrary large scenes.
